QR Restaurant Menu web application


In response to COVID-19 measures, and with the intention of making an environmental friendly alternative to disposable restaurant menus, I developed a web application which is triggered by scanning the QR code printed on weather proof stickers.


  • Design
  • Development
  • Mobile/tablet responsiveness
  • Stickers design
  • Leaflets design
  • Custom JS animations
