I am Malta based visual artist.


My coding route


Started learning about web development 3 years ago. After 2 years of self learning and developing, I decided to step forward and do the official certification at Institute of Computer Education.


Today, my main focus is creation of resposive websites and web applications, implementing the UI and UX principles.


Templates and website builders are simply not my thing. Everything I create is purely custom tailored, designed and developed by me.


Photography


It's hard to determine when I started this path but I guess it would be accurate to say that it was in me since early age. From old school film cameras to modern DSLRs.


I love photography because it gives you power to freeze a moment in time, while time moves on and the moment dissappears. Regardless of the subject, I don't shoot what it looks like, I'm trying to present the idea of what it feels like.
